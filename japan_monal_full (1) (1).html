✈️ Land · Clear Immigration (~30–40 min)
  • Buy Suica IC Card at the airport — works on ALL metro, JR, buses & convenience stores. Load ¥5,000 to start.
  • Narita: Take the Narita Express (N'EX) → Shinjuku Station (~80 min, ¥3,070). Then JR Sobu-Chuo Line (local yellow train, westbound platform) → Iidabashi Station (2 stops, 5 min, ¥170)
  • Haneda: Take Keikyu Airport Express → Nihombashi Station (~45 min, ¥650). Then Tokyo Metro Tozai Line → Iidabashi Station (4 stops westbound, 8 min, ¥180)
~1:30 PM
🏨 Hotel MYSTAYS Iidabashi — Check In & Rest
  • Exit Iidabashi Station West Exit (B3 exit) → 3 min walk to hotel
  • Drop bags, freshen up · Early check-in subject to availability
~2:30 PM
🚂 Iidabashi → Harajuku Station (JR Sobu-Chuo Line)
  • Iidabashi Station → JR Sobu-Chuo Line (local yellow train, Platform 5, direction Mitaka/Shinjuku)
  • Stations: Iidabashi → Ichigaya → Yotsuya → Shinanomachi → Sendagaya → Harajuku
  • 5 stops · ~15 min · ¥180
  • From Harajuku Station: Walk 2 min north through the torii gate → Meiji Shrine
~2:45 PM
⛩️ Meiji Shrine — Visit First (Beat the Crowd!)
  • Inner Garden — Kiyomasa's Well (power spot 🌿) · Treasure House — Imperial artifacts
  • 🧧 Buy Red Omamori charm (luck & protection) · Write wishes on wooden Ema plaques
~4:30 PM
🛍️ Harajuku — Takeshita Street (Cross the bridge from Harajuku Stn)
  • 📱 Cas Pace  📍 · 🏠 Dear You Homewares  📍

~5:45 PM
🚃 Harajuku → Shibuya (JR Yamanote Line · 1 stop)

  • 1 stop · 2 min · ¥150  ·  OR simply walk south 15 min via Omotesando
~6:00 PM
🌆 Shibuya Sky — Pre-Book (4–6 PM golden hour ideal)
  • Located in Shibuya Scramble Square Tower — take elevator inside the building
  • 📸 Tip: Starbucks Shibuya Tsutaya (2F, overlooking crossing) = best street-level Shibuya shot!
  • Alt: Shibuya Magnet Rooftop Bar — buy a drink, free view  📍 · OR Tokyo Gov. Building (totally free + night show)
  • 🎫 Book Shibuya Sky Here
